The Problem

  • Teachers lose valuable time to attendance, grading, reporting, and daily administration.
  • Schools face increasing NEP 2020 documentation and compliance requirements.
  • Tier 2, Tier 3, and rural schools struggle with cloud-only tools because internet access is unreliable.
  • Paper-based school operations create cost, delay, and poor visibility for management.

The Solution

EduPortal is the cloud school operating platform for admins, auditors, principals, HODs, teachers, students, and parents.

EduOS is the offline-first edge layer designed to keep key school operations running locally during internet outages and sync safely with the cloud after reconnection.

Core Capabilities

40%teacher admin workload reduction target
90%paper reduction potential through digitisation
NEPcompliance dashboards and reports
OfflineEduOS sync architecture for low-connectivity schools
  • Role-based school dashboards, academic operations, attendance workflows, assignments, digital records, study materials, and student progress tracking.
  • AI-assisted grading, OCR, content generation, and teacher productivity tools.
  • NEP 2020 compliance modules, audit-ready reporting, analytics, and institutional visibility.
  • EduOS-ready offline-sync architecture for future hardware field deployment.

Current Status

The software MVP is built and undergoing internal trial and demo validation. The next milestone is limited EduOS hardware field deployment, which requires funding for pilot units, field testing, security review, and real-school validation.

Business Model

  • Annual SaaS subscriptions for schools.
  • Optional EduOS hardware packages.
  • Setup, training, and deployment services.
  • Premium AI, analytics, and compliance modules.
