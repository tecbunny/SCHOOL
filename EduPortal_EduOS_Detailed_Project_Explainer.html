Project summary: EduPortal & EduOS is built to help Indian schools digitise daily operations without depending completely on always-on internet. The platform is designed for school administration, teachers, students, compliance teams, and future EduOS hardware-supported offline operation.

1. The Problem In Indian Schools

Many Indian schools want to digitise, but the reality on the ground is difficult. Teachers handle attendance, grading, lesson planning, reports, student records, communication, and compliance work alongside teaching. This reduces classroom focus and creates pressure on school staff.

At the same time, NEP 2020 has increased the need for structured academic records, Holistic Progress Card style reporting, institutional visibility, and audit-ready compliance. In many Tier 2, Tier 3, and rural schools, cloud-only SaaS platforms are hard to depend on because internet availability is inconsistent.

Teacher Workload

  • Manual attendance and classroom records.
  • Manual grading and assignment checking.
  • Time-consuming report preparation.
  • Limited time for mentoring and teaching quality.

School Infrastructure Gap

  • Unreliable internet in many regions.
  • Paper-based workflows and fragmented records.
  • Low visibility for principals and administrators.
  • Difficulty adopting cloud-only education platforms.

2. Mission

Our mission is to help digitise the Indian education system by giving schools a modern platform where teachers can focus on teaching, administration becomes automated, and Indian students become comfortable learning, growing, and participating on digital platforms.

3. The Solution

EduPortal & EduOS is designed as a hybrid cloud-edge education platform. EduPortal acts as the central school operating system in the cloud, while EduOS is the offline-first local infrastructure layer planned for school environments where connectivity is weak or unreliable.

EduPortal: Cloud Command Center

EduPortal gives each stakeholder a role-based workspace. Administrators can manage institutions and users, principals and HODs can monitor school performance, teachers can manage academic workflows, and students can access assignments, study hubs, assessments, and digital records.

  • Admin and school dashboards.
  • Teacher workspace and student portal.
  • Auditor and compliance views.
  • Attendance, assignments, timetables, study materials, and records.

EduOS: Offline-First Infrastructure

EduOS is the planned local edge layer for schools. Its purpose is to allow key school operations to continue locally during internet outages and sync safely with the cloud when internet returns.

  • Local school operation during connectivity gaps.
  • Offline-sync architecture prepared for field validation.
  • Student Hub and Class Station deployment concept.
  • Future hardware pilot units funded through this round.

6. Market Opportunity

India has a very large school ecosystem, with strong pressure to digitise education, improve student records, reduce teacher workload, and align with NEP 2020 expectations. The most practical first wedge is not every school immediately, but schools that urgently need digitisation and cannot depend on stable cloud access.

The initial target segment is Tier 2 and Tier 3 private schools, rural schools supported through CSR programs, school chains looking for operational standardisation, and institutions needing compliance automation.

Initial Target Customers

7. Business Model

EduPortal & EduOS is designed as a combined software, hardware, and services model. After validation, schools can move into annual subscriptions with optional EduOS hardware packages and premium AI/compliance modules.

Revenue Stream Description
Annual SaaS Subscription Recurring school platform subscription based on institution size, modules, and usage.
EduOS Hardware Packages Optional local edge hardware for schools needing offline-first operation and local classroom access.
Setup And Training Deployment, onboarding, school staff training, configuration, and support services.
Premium Modules AI-assisted grading/content tools, analytics, compliance automation, and advanced institutional reporting.
